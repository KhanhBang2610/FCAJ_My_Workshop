1. Worklog

An overview of the internship and the development of the Smart Media Analytics project, broken down into weekly milestones with specific objectives:

  • Week 1: AWS Basics: Familiarized with the AWS ecosystem, practiced core services, and surveyed project directions.
  • Week 2: Lambda & VPC: Researched and configured Amazon VPC network infrastructure, Subnets, and AWS Lambda.
  • Week 3: Project Orientation: Onboarded at the company, learned internal workflows, and defined the project direction.
  • Week 4: Backend & Docker: Initialized the FastAPI Backend structure, practiced Docker packaging, and explored ECR/ECS Fargate.
  • Week 5: Database & API: Integrated PostgreSQL and Redis databases, and finalized the design of core management APIs.
  • Week 6: AI Pipeline & Testing: Deployed AI services via Docker, synchronized the development environment, and fully tested the Backend.
  • Week 7: Vector Store & Media: Completed the asynchronous AI pipeline (Scene, Image Caption, Transcription) and integrated ChromaDB.
  • Week 8: Frontend Init: Built the Frontend platform with React, managed centralized States, and made the first API connections.
  • Week 9: Bedrock & WebSocket: Connected AWS Bedrock, handled real-time uploads via WebSocket, and optimized Vector Embeddings.
  • Week 10: Dashboard & Audio: Built the Audio Waveform, finalized the interactive Dashboard, and adjusted AWS architecture based on feedback.
  • Week 11: End-to-End & UI/UX: Connected the E2E AI pipeline, packaged for Fargate, and overhauled the UI to an App-like standard.
  • Week 12: CI/CD & Deployment: Automated CI/CD, set up CloudWatch monitoring, and completed the comprehensive A-Z Workshop documentation.
